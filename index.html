Study Title: Intelligent Incident Reporting System

1. Statement and Purpose

You are invited to participate in a research study titled above. The purpose of this study is to validate a web-based incident reporting interface. We are testing the usability of the map plotting system and the accuracy of the manual reporting features under simulated conditions.

2. Participation & Procedure

Your participation involves acting as a Beta Tester. You will be asked to:

  • Access the web interface on your device.
  • Manually navigate the map to specific target locations defined in your mission objectives.
  • Drop pins to mark simulated infrastructure issues (e.g., Potholes, Floods).

Duration: Approximately 20 minutes.

3. Data Collection & Privacy

For the purpose of system analysis, the application will record:

  • Pin Location: The specific geographic coordinates (Latitude/Longitude) of the markers you place on the map.
  • Report Details: The category and description text you enter.
  • Timestamps: The exact time you submit each report.

Important Privacy Note: This application does not track your real-time device location or movement history. It only records the specific points you choose to mark on the map. Your personal identity is not stored publicly; you will be identified only by a generic Tester ID in the database.

4. Potential Risks & Benefits

Risks: There are no known risks. Participation is virtual via the web interface.

Benefits: You will help improve a Smart City technology designed to manage urban infrastructure more effectively.

5. Voluntary Participation

Participation is completely voluntary. You may stop the test or close the browser at any time without penalty.
